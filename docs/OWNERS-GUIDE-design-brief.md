# Owner's Guide — design brief

**What it is.** A one-page printed reference sheet that ships in the box with *Ground Truth*, a
small e-paper desk display that shows live regional earthquakes. It's a gift — warm, not corporate.

**Audience.** Computer-comfortable (a PC gamer) but not a specialist. Clear and scannable beats
exhaustive.

**Source files.**
- `OWNERS-GUIDE.md` — the words. Edit these; don't rewrite the instructions.
- `OWNERS-GUIDE.html` — a rough print mock for reference/layout only (not the final design).

**Format.**
- **One side of US Letter, portrait** — must fit a single page on a home inkjet/laser.
- **Ink-light** (mostly black on white, minimal color) so it's cheap to print.
- Two columns fit the content; not required.

**Please preserve (functional, not stylistic):**
- The **section order**, and every literal address/credential shown in monospace:
  `GroundTruth-Setup`, `groundtruth.local`, `192.168.4.1`, `/update`, `admin`, and the MAC addresses.
- The **"This device" box** — the two password lines are filled by hand per device (leave them
  blank); the MAC addresses are real and stay exactly as shown.
- Don't reword the technical steps.

**Free to design:**
- Typography, hierarchy, section markers, the title treatment, a tasteful earth / seismograph motif.
- A spot under the title for an optional one-line personal dedication.

**Tone.** Friendly, gift-like, confident — not a datasheet.
